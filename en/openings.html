Open Positions

Students and postdoctoral researchers interested in condensed matter theory and computational materials are welcome to contact us.

Who we are recruiting

PhD students, master students, undergraduate research interns, and postdoctoral researchers.

Curiosity, persistence, and willingness to learn are valued. Prior programming or simulation experience is helpful but not mandatory for junior students.

How to apply

Please send a CV and a brief statement of research interests to lflin@utk.edu.
