Intelligent Quantum Materials Lab (IQM Lab)

IQM Lab studies strongly correlated and quantum materials using first-principles calculations, model simulations, and many-body numerical methods, with interests in superconductivity, magnetism, ferroelectricity, and multiferroicity.
