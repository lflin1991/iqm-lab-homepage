Research Areas

We connect electronic structure, effective models, and emergent quantum phenomena.

Superconductivity and correlated materials

Nickelates, cuprates, and transition-metal compounds; electronic structure, magnetic order, orbital physics, and pairing mechanisms.

Magnetism and multiferroicity

Exchange interactions, charge/orbital ordering, magnetoelectric coupling, and noncollinear ferroelectricity in oxides and fluorides.

Computational materials theory

First-principles calculations, Wannier-based modeling, model Hamiltonians, and many-body numerical simulations.
